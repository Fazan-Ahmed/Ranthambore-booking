Best Time to Visit Ranthambore National Park in 2026

Ranthambore National Park is one of India's most famous tiger reserves, located in Rajasthan. Every year thousands of wildlife lovers visit Ranthambore to experience thrilling tiger safaris and explore the rich biodiversity of the park.

The best time to visit Ranthambore depends on your travel goals. Some visitors come for tiger sightings, while others prefer pleasant weather and bird watching opportunities.

Best Season to Visit Ranthambore

The park remains open from October to June every year. During this period tourists can enjoy jeep safari and canter safari experiences in different safari zones.

  • October to February: Pleasant weather and bird watching.
  • March to April: Good balance of weather and tiger sightings.
  • May to June: Highest chance of tiger sightings.

Winter Season (October to February)

Winter is considered the most comfortable season to visit Ranthambore. Temperatures range from 8°C to 25°C, making safari rides enjoyable.

This season is ideal for families, photographers and bird watchers. The landscape looks beautiful after the monsoon and wildlife activity remains high throughout the day.

Summer Season (March to June)

Summer is the best time for tiger sightings. As water sources become limited, tigers frequently visit lakes and waterholes, increasing the chances of spotting them.

Temperatures can exceed 40°C, so morning safaris are highly recommended. Despite the heat, wildlife enthusiasts consider this the most rewarding season for photography.

Safari Timings in Ranthambore

Safari timings change throughout the year depending on sunrise and sunset. Visitors can choose between morning and afternoon safari slots.
